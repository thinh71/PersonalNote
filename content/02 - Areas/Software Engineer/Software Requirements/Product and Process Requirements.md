- **A product requirement**: is a *need* or *constraint* on the software to be developed. 	
E.g: The software shall verify that a student meets all prerequisites before he or she registers for a course. 

- A process requirement: is essentially a constraint on the development of the software.

E.g: The software shall be developed using a a QMS process
